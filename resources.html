Resources

Preparation

The best quality results derive from the best experimental setup. To ensure that the samples we receive are of the highest quality, we provide our users and the scientific community with user guides for each facility, protocols for sample preparation and analysis, and reagents that will assist them with performing the optimal sample preparation prior to analysis. We also host seminars, workshops and provide in-facility training to inform users on how they may apply our services to their biological question. If you have questions related to our services that are not answered in this resource, please contact the applicable Facility Manager.
